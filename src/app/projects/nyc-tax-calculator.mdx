This project is a simple tool to help you estimate your annual income taxes in New York City. It takes into account federal, state, and city tax brackets, and allows you to include common deductions to get a more accurate estimate.

The calculator also has a special section for self-employed individuals, allowing them to include deductions for health insurance premiums and other business expenses.
